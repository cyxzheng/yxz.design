My caffeine addiction

I'm the weird coffee person in my circle

I'm nowhere as weird or knowledgeable as the original weird coffee person, James Hoffmann, but considering I utilize two scales when making my coffee (one to weigh the raw input of beans, one to weigh the output of coffee), I'm probably weirder than the average coffee drinker.

I don't actually like drinking straight espresso though. Maybe I can't dial it in right. But I always prefer a milk drink, like a cappuccino. Making latte art is what makes coffee enjoyable for me.
